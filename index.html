COMING SOON

2020

From Nihil

O builders of temples made of dust
Watering the land with tears and blood
You, who have raised your ephemeral existence
Above every other else's…

O worshippers of cinder-tasting icons
Enthralled by a mystified destiny
You, who have deemed that every single thing in the world
Was a mere present thrown at your feet…

Haul your fingers from this chaos spawn treasures
And prepare to embrace the void…

O insignificant creatures
Now has come the time for you to give way…
For absolute fate
Was never the question…

EP Duality

Here he comes
He rises again within the depths of my entrails… jaws of iron
I can feel him, he knows it
He is what i am
My soul, my body, my heart, everything belongs to him

They dance before us… rag dolls
He knows it, they can feel him
I am what he is
And our gestures draw the outlines of their lives, everything belongs to him

My mind expends and implodes… bubbles of cotton
I know it, he can feel it
I want to be what he is
And our voices tune up to silence theirs, everything belongs to him

This vision is so wonderful… waterfall of salt
You know it, you can feel it
You are what we are
And our flesh unites theirs, everything belongs to us

The journey is almost over… carousel of dust
You know it, everything can be felt
They are what they are
Their lives and feelings are nothing but an illusion, everything belongs to us

The night is brighter and brighter… temple of granite
We knew it, everything has grown silent
I am alone and you are gone
Your soul, your body, your heart… from now on, everything belongs to me

The Grey

Can you hear the sound of the world?
This ephemeral echo that slowly burns through the void…
Can you see beyond the veil?
This overwhelming presence that enhances the truthfulness of any reality…
And then…
When everything has fade away and the world is silent…
All there will be left is silence…
And the grey
